Qualifications for CDL Drivers

FreightFlow hiring standards require all driver applicants to be a minimum of 25 years of age and have a minimum of 2 years verifiable tractor trailer driving experience within the United States and/or Canada in similar equipment. (FMCSR, Sec. 391.11 (1)). Candidates with more than six jobs in the last three years could be rejected.

Driver applicants with 3 or fewer chargeable or preventable motor vehicle accidents within the past 3 years will be considered for employment. Because of Top Team Carrier’s commitment to safety, driver applicants who fail to meet the above standard will not be considered for employment by the company.

Disqualification

FreightFlow is committed to maintaining the highest standards of safety and reliability. Driver applicants may be disqualified if they have a history of unsafe driving behavior, license suspensions, or violations related to drugs or alcohol within the past five years.

Applicants with felony convictions involving a vehicle, violent offenses, or theft may also be disqualified. Any falsification of information during the hiring process will result in automatic disqualification and removal from consideration for employment with Top Team Carrier.
